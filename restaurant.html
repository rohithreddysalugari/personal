OUR STORY

Our approach is simple; “food has to be tasty, simple and customers have to enjoy” We rely on repeated customers exemplifies our focus on the quality of food. We are very popular in Hyderabad and getting recognized in Warangal, and serve mouthwatering food at the highest standards with best price to customers in our restaurants and special parties outside. Our grill experts are butchers by profession hailing from Telangana state, also known as land of famous “Kohinoor” diamond. Grilling the meat on a wood fired charcoal grill has been their specialty for generations. The grilling process consists of many steps like selecting the right Sheep/Chicken/Fish, cutting the meat delicately, marinating the same using the perfectly balanced sauce and grilling the meat is a very challenging and immensely satisfying experience.
